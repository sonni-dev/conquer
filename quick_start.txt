üß† THE ADHD/DEPRESSION DESIGN:

- **Variable Energy**: Some days making tea is 1 step, some days it's 4. 
  The system gets it.

- **No All-or-Nothing**: Don't let perfect be the enemy of done. Low energy 
  completion still earns XP and maintains your streak.

- **Category Balance**: See if you're neglecting self-care, social connection, 
  or other life areas. No judgment - just awareness.

- **Choose When Completing**: Even if you filtered for "high energy" tasks, 
  you can still pick the low energy version when completing if the task 
  felt harder than expected.

- **Streak**: Complete at least ONE task per day (any tier!) to maintain
